

\chapter*{Appendix A - List of eVTOL passenger vehicles in development}

\setcounter{page}{1}

While there are no eVTOL passenger vehicles currently in service, there are a number of projects in development. The following list has been compiled from various sources\cite{evtolconfig}\cite{evtolreview}, including manufacturers websites, and evtol.news website\footnote{\url{http://evtol.news/aircraft/} [accessed on 13 May, 2019]}. While it is not possible to verify the performance claims of all of these vehicles, care has been taken to exclude unreliable sources and outliers.
